Red Rising

Red Rising is a dystopian sci-fi novel by Pierce Brown about a 16-year-old named Darrow who lives in a color-coded society on Mars in the distant future The story takes place in a solar system where humans have colonized planets and created color-based social castes. The Golds are at the top of the social hierarchy, while the Reds are the lowest caste. Darrow is a Red who works as a miner on Mars, extracting helium-3 to terraform the planet. Darrow believes he is working toward a better future for humanity, but discovers that Mars has been inhabited for generations by the Golds, who exploit the Reds as slave labor. Darrow is rescued by a group of rebels who disguise him as a Gold and infiltrate the Golds' command school. Darrow's goal is to take down his oppressors from within. Some say the book's premise is similar to The Hunger Games or Battle Royale. Others say the book is intense, brutal, and addictive. A board game based on the book is available. Pierce Brown is also working with screenwriters to adapt Red Rising into a movie
